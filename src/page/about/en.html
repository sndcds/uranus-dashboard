Discover culture with Uranus

Uranus helps you quickly and easily discover events, places, and cultural offerings – from small local initiatives to major cultural institutions.

All events are displayed equally, regardless of size or budget. This way, you can also find things that are often overlooked. With filters, you can search specifically for events that interest you.

Uranus is developed as open-source software by the community. This creates a growing, independent overview of cultural diversity.
