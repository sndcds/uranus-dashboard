Find events, places, and cultural offerings

There are countless cultural offerings to discover – some right in your neighborhood, others in different districts or regions. From small local initiatives and associations to independent cultural creators, museums, theaters, and large cultural institutions. kulturbytes aims to help you discover new things, browse around, or find exactly what you are looking for.

Our goal is to make cultural information more accessible and to bring the diversity of cultural offerings together in one place. To achieve this, we provide a platform where cultural institutions, projects, and event organizers can publish their offerings. Visitors can search, filter, and explore this information – free of charge and without barriers.

For everyone. Free of charge.

kulturbytes is a free and open service for everyone. Using the platform is free – both for people who want to discover culture and for institutions and initiatives that want to make their offerings visible.

Making small institutions visible

Especially smaller institutions, associations, initiatives, and independent cultural creators often do not have the same opportunities for digital visibility as large institutions.
kulturbytes therefore explicitly aims to provide a platform for those who work with limited resources but make an important contribution to cultural life. The diversity of a cultural landscape is not created by large institutions alone, but equally by the many committed people and projects on the ground.

From the community for the community

kulturbytes is developed on a voluntary basis and released as open-source software. The project started in November 2025 at OK Lab Flensburg.

We believe that digital infrastructure for culture should be open, transparent, and developed collaboratively. Therefore, anyone can understand how the platform works, contribute improvements, or directly participate in its further development. This creates a growing, independent overview of cultural diversity – step by step, from the community for the community.

Open, diverse, and inclusive

kulturbytes understands itself as a platform for culture and cultural participation.

Party-political positions, political opinion shaping, or societal polarization are not the focus of this service. Our focus is on making cultural activities visible and enabling exchange about cultural offerings.

We stand for a diverse, open, and democratic cultural landscape. kulturbytes aims to make cultural participation easier for everyone, regardless of origin, age, gender, identity, disability, religion, or worldview.

Respect, openness, and mutual appreciation form the foundation of how we work together.
