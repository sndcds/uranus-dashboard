Getting Started

This guide explains how to create your own organization with a venue, optional rooms, and an event. This structure forms the foundation for nearly all features in Uranus. Therefore, it is important to provide complete and high-quality information for each area.

Important: Most information you enter in Uranus is publicly visible. You will be explicitly informed when data is used internally only.

Note: Uranus can also be used without creating your own organization. In this case, you will be invited as a team member by an existing organization.

Registration

  1. Click [Log in] in the menu bar.
  2. At the bottom, select [Register].
  3. Enter your email address and a password. For your security, choose a password with at least 8 characters, ideally including numbers and special characters.
  4. Click [Create account].
  5. You will receive an email at the provided address.
    Open the email and activate your account using the included link.
  6. After successful activation, you can log in.

Log in

  1. Open the following link in your web browser: https://uranus.oklabflensburg.de/app/login
  2. Enter your email address and password, then click [Log in].
  3. After logging in, you will see the portal page displaying public events.
  4. In the top-right menu, you can access settings such as language, password, and personal profile data.
  5. Click [Dashboard] in the menu to access your personal workspace.
    On the left, you will see all areas where you can work.

Create an Organization

  1. Switch to the [Organizations] workspace.
  2. Click [Create organization].
  3. Fill in at least all fields marked with *.
    You can update or extend the information at any time.
    Important: Only create organizations you are genuinely responsible for. Organizations provide the legal and organizational basis for venues, events, and teams.
  4. Click [Save].
  5. You will now see the overview or info card for your organization.
  6. Click [Activate] to start working on behalf of this organization.

Add a Venue

  1. Select the [Venues] workspace.
  2. Click [Add venue].
  3. Complete at least all required fields marked with *.
    The information can be adjusted later.
    Important: Only create venues that are managed by the currently active organization. Venues represent the physical locations where events take place.
  4. Click [Save].

Add Rooms (optional)

  1. Open an existing venue.
  2. Navigate to the [Rooms] section.
  3. Click [Add room].
  4. Enter the roomâ€™s name, description, and optionally its capacity.
  5. Save the room. Rooms can later be assigned to events or event dates.

Add an Event

  1. Select the [Events] workspace.
  2. Click [New event].
  3. Enter the title, description, image, and other relevant information.
    An event describes the overall concept of the activity (e.g. a concert or exhibition).
  4. Create at least one event date with date, time, and venue.
  5. Click [Save] to publish the event or save it as a draft.

Glossary

  • User: A registered person with an active user account.
  • Organization: A legal or organizational entity that operates venues and hosts events. Organizations can have teams.
  • Venue: A physical location for events (e.g. theater, club, gallery). A venue can contain multiple rooms.
  • Room: A single event space within a venue.
  • Event: An activity with descriptions, images, and metadata (e.g. concert, reading, exhibition).
  • Event date: A specific date and time when an event takes place. An event may have one or multiple dates.
  • Dashboard: Your personal workspace for managing organizations, venues, events, and teams.
  • Team: A group of users working together within an organization, with different roles and permissions.
